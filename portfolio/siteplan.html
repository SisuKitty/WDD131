Portfolio Site Plan

Sheena Gibbons

WDD 131-A1

Overview

Purpose

To provide a place for potential employers and clients to be able to understand my skillset and view the designs/art I have created which portray my skills. Along with a place for other artists to be inspired by my work and be able to reach out for collaboration.

Audience

The audience is potential employers and clients.

Dynamic elements

The images will react to hovering and when clicked will pull up a window displaying the projects process and details. Along with content filtering for the viewer to specify what they are looking for.

Navigation

Home Portfolio About Contact

Content

Home page

Introduce with my name and a clear intro to my work with a motion reel. Next would lead into some of the best portfolio work, with a button to be able to look at all. Then connections to the page that tells them more details about me and finally an invite to contact me to work with me.

Portfolio

A streamlined way to look at my designs and a feature of being able to filter the work to display what the viewer is most interested in seeing. First showing it all, then can filter between motion, branding&identity, UX/UI/web, illustration, photography, etc.

About

An edited photo of myself with flourishes of art in the photo. Then an introduction to myself and my passions. Then a list of what I am good at and additional skills.

Contact

Preferred form of contact displayed first, which would be by my email. Then a option to fill out a form with a request for creating a design, with a form to wakl them through the details needed to determine if I can do that for them.

Wireframes

Landing Page

The first thing seen will be a motion reel to show off my work dynamically. Then sections to direct to the rest of the website's contants like looking more into my portfolio and learning more about the designer. Then each page at the footer will encourage reaching out to the designer.The navigation will be automatically open but when closed, the name will shrink and the content will be centered.

Portfolio Page

As you hover over the gallery of images it will display the name of the one you are hovering on along with an animation. When the image is clicked, it will bring up a window to display the details & process of the project.

About Page

As you scroll the sections will appear and the title image will be a fun whimsical Photoshop edited photo of the designer.

Contant Page

The primary form of contact will be front and center and clicking will pull up an email, but there is then a form for sending an inquiry to the designer about any possible projects, jobs or collaborations.
